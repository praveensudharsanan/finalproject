Want to Become a Featured Designer?

Join Our Network of Fabulous Fashion Designers and Influencers!

At Custom Stitches, we pride ourselves in being a hub for new and upcoming designers. With our vast worldwide network of amazing designers and fasion icons, we are able to bring you the connections that are necessary to break into the world of fashion.

If you think you have what it takes to become the next fashion icon and want to partner with Custom Stitches to get there, apply to become the newest member of our network!

How To Apply

To apply, we need all applicants to fill out the application form and submit a portfolio that includes at least five images of your original fashion designs, up to three photos of you having a great time doing something you love (which can include zany selfies) and a short blurb about what fashion means to you.
